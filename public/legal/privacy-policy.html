1. Scope and Controller

This Privacy Policy explains how ParlerAI ("ParlerAI", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data when you use our websites, mobile apps, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

For data protection questions, you can contact us at contact@parlerai.app.

2. Data We Process

Depending on how you use the Service, we may process:

  • Input content, such as words, phrases, and text submitted for lookup or translation.
  • Language selections, app settings, and feature preferences.
  • Technical and usage data (for example: IP address, user agent, timestamps, and request metadata).
  • Support communications you send to us.
  • Purchase metadata required for subscription and In-App Purchase verification (without storing full card data).

3. How We Use Data

We use personal data to:

  • Provide dictionary and translation functionality.
  • Operate, maintain, troubleshoot, and secure the Service.
  • Enforce rate limits, fraud prevention, and abuse detection.
  • Process subscriptions and In-App Purchases through platform providers.
  • Improve product quality and user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and handle lawful requests.

4. GDPR Legal Bases (EEA/UK)

Where GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we process personal data under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Contract necessity: to provide the Service you request.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure, improve, and operate our Service responsibly.
  • Consent: where required, including analytics cookies and similar technologies.
  • Legal obligation: where processing is required by law.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and local storage technologies. Analytics cookies are loaded only after you provide consent in the GDPR banner. If you reject, analytics scripts are not loaded.

Essential storage may still be used for core app functionality (for example, language settings, legal consent status, and local history features).

6. Local Storage and Device Data

Some data is stored on your device/browser to improve performance and continuity, including:

  • App language and target-language settings.
  • Query history and cached lookup results.
  • Consent preferences for legal and analytics banners.

You can clear this data using your browser or app storage controls.

7. In-App Purchases and Payment Data

For iOS, payments are processed by Apple App Store. For Android, payments are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store full payment card numbers.

We may process transaction status, subscription identifiers, and entitlement metadata needed to activate and manage paid features.

8. Data Sharing and Recipients

We may share data with:

  • Infrastructure and cloud service providers acting as processors under contractual safeguards.
  • Analytics providers, only where consent is provided when required.
  • Platform operators (Apple/Google) for billing and subscription management.
  • Professional advisors, auditors, and authorities where legally required.

We do not sell personal data in exchange for monetary consideration.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your jurisdiction. Where required, we use recognized transfer safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), and supplementary protections.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this Policy, including service delivery, security, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements.

Retention periods vary based on data type, sensitivity, and legal requirements.

11. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data, including access control, logging, and transport security where appropriate.

No security system is perfect. You should also protect your own device, account credentials, and browser environment.

12. Your Privacy Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of data.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Request data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing relies on consent.

You can submit rights requests by contacting contact@parlerai.app. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

13. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age threshold defined by local law. If you believe a child has provided personal data unlawfully, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

14. Automated Decision-Making

We do not use solely automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects about users in the sense of GDPR Article 22.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected in the app or website with an updated effective date.

Your continued use after updates means you acknowledge the revised policy.
